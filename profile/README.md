## 🧠 Overview

**Boxcutter** is a high-performance hard surface modeling plugin designed to enhance and accelerate boolean workflows in Blender. It provides artists with an intuitive and interactive way to create complex shapes using cutting, slicing, and procedural operations directly in the viewport.

Focused on speed and precision, Boxcutter allows users to draw shapes in real time and instantly apply boolean operations such as cuts, unions, and insets. This makes it especially effective for designing mechanical parts, sci-fi assets, and detailed hard surface models without relying on time-consuming manual techniques.

The plugin integrates seamlessly into Blender’s interface, offering a smooth and responsive workflow that feels natural for both new users and experienced professionals. With customizable controls and non-destructive workflows, users can experiment freely while maintaining full control over their geometry.

Boxcutter works particularly well alongside other modeling tools and addons, enabling a flexible pipeline for asset creation. Its ability to quickly block out and refine shapes makes it a valuable asset for concept artists and production teams alike.

Whether you're creating game-ready assets, product visualizations, or complex 3D designs, **Boxcutter** provides the tools needed to work faster and more efficiently.
